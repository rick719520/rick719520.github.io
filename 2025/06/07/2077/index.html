Night City Career Hub

BREAK THE LIMITS. FORGE YOUR LEGEND.

Welcome to the datastream, choom. Welcome to the Night City Career Hub—a fully immersive, cyberpunk-themed web platform designed as an intelligent employment service for the modern age. In a world where legends aren’t born but built, this hub is your gateway to high-stakes gigs and career ascendancy.

This project transforms the traditional job search into a high-tech, gamified experience, heavily inspired by the world of Cyberpunk 2077. From the glitchy, neon-drenched UI to the genre-specific copywriting, every element is crafted to pull the user into the dystopian metropolis of Night City.

// CORE FEATURES_

The platform is comprised of three main areas:

  1. Identity Creation (register.html):

    • New users don’t just sign up; they forge a new identity.
    • Create a unique Callsign and choose a starting Protocol: the rebellious Solo or the strategic Corpo-Agent.
    • The system adapts, requesting specific profile data based on your chosen faction, such as your Chip_ID and Skillset or your Corporate allegiance and business sector.

  2. System Access (login.html):

    • Returning users don’t just log in; they establish a secure link to the data core.
    • The interface provides direct access using a registered Access ID and Access Key.

  3. The Hub (index.html):

    • City Broadcast: A real-time feed of city-wide alerts, high-priority bounties, and underground trade offers, keeping you plugged into the ever-shifting landscape of Night City.
    • Corp-Gigs: Find your next job by browsing corporate commissions, from megacorporations like Arasaka and Militech to street-level fixer gigs.
    • Chrome Up (Skill Enhancement): This is more than a resume builder. Optimize your identity, simulate interviews in the Braindance Hub, and browse skill trees to become the legend you were meant to be.
    • Access Point: Manage your personal databank, track tagged gigs, and monitor your quest progress.
    • Advanced Services: Interface directly with intelligence fixers, reconstruct your personality matrix, overclock your implants for faster reaction times, or even pre-simulate missions in the safety of a braindance.

// AESTHETICS & TECHNOLOGY_

  • UI/UX: The entire platform features a dark, neon-infused design with glitch animations, scanlines, and custom iconography to create an authentic cyberpunk feel. UI elements like buttons, forms, and navigation bars use custom clip-paths and hover effects for a high-tech, angular look.
  • Frontend: The site is built with standard HTML5, CSS3, and modern vanilla JavaScript, ensuring a responsive and lightweight experience without legacy dependencies like jQuery.
  • Frameworks: Utilizes Bootstrap 5 for a solid responsive grid system, enhanced with extensive custom CSS to achieve its unique aesthetic.

Jack in, and let’s get to work.

夜之城职业中心

打破限制。铸就传奇。

欢迎来到数据流，choom。欢迎来到夜之城职业中心——一个完全沉浸式的、赛博朋克主题的网络平台，为现代社会设计的智能就业服务。在这个传奇并非与生俱来，而是靠自己打造的世界里，这里是你通往高风险委托和职业晋升的门户。

本项目将传统的求职过程转变为一种高科技、游戏化的体验，其灵感主要来源于赛博朋克2077的世界。从充满毛刺效果、霓虹泛滥的UI到特定类型的文案，每一个元素都经过精心设计，旨在将用户带入反乌托邦的大都会——夜之城。

// 核心功能_

平台由三个主要区域组成：

  1. 身份创建 (register.html):

    • 新用户不只是注册，他们是伪造一个新的身份。
    • 创建一个独特的呼号，并选择一个初始协议：叛逆的独狼(Solo)或策略性的公司特工(Corpo-Agent)。
    • 系统会根据你选择的阵营，自适应地要求特定的档案数据，例如你的芯片ID和技能专长，或你的公司背景和业务部门。

  2. 系统接入 (login.html):

    • 返回的用户不只是登录，他们是建立一个到数据核心的安全链接。
    • 该界面使用注册的接入ID和访问密钥提供直接访问。

  3. 中心 (index.html):

    • 城市广播: 实时更新的全市警报、高优先级赏金和地下交易信息，让你随时接入夜之城瞬息万变的动态。
    • 公司委托: 通过浏览企业委托来寻找你的下一份工作，从像荒坂和军用科技这样的巨型企业到街头中间人发布的委托。
    • 义体强化 (技能提升): 这不仅仅是一个简历构建器。优化你的身份，在超梦（Braindance）中心模拟面试，并浏览技能树，成为你命中注定的传奇。
    • 接入点: 管理你的个人数据库，跟踪已标记的委托，并监控你的任务进度。
    • 高级服务: 直接与情报掮客对接，重构你的人格矩阵，为更快的反应时间超频你的植入体，甚至在超梦的安全环境中预演任务。

// 美学与技术_

  • UI/UX: 整个平台采用黑暗、霓虹灯浸染的设计，带有故障艺术动画、扫描线和自定义图标，营造出真实的赛博朋克氛围。UI元素（如按钮、表单和导航栏）使用自定义的裁切路径和悬停效果，打造出高科技、棱角分明的外观。
  • 前端: 网站采用标准的HTML5、CSS3和现代原生JavaScript构建，确保了轻量级、响应迅速的体验，并且没有像jQuery那样的过时依赖。
  • 框架: 利用Bootstrap 5提供坚实的响应式网格系统，并通过大量自定义CSS进行增强，以实现其独特的美学。
